## Preparation Checklist

Before starting a new university engagement:

- [ ] Complete deep research on leadership (this profile)
- [ ] Identify strategic priorities from annual reports
- [ ] Map decision-making structure
- [ ] Register on procurement portal
- [ ] Find 2-3 warm introductions via LinkedIn
- [ ] Prepare case studies relevant to their challenges
- [ ] Draft initial value proposition
